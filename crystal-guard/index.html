Controls

Click any empty grass tile to build a tower. Click an existing tower to Upgrade or Sell it.

Strategy

Archers are great for bosses. Mages slow down fast enemies. Cannons destroy groups. Place Support towers in the middle of clusters!

The Goal

Enemies will try to reach the Blue Crystal. Defeat them to earn Gold. If 20 enemies reach the crystal, it shatters!
